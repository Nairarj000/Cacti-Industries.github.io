Meet the Team

The people behind Cacti Industries

Frank Regal

CEO and Co-Founder

Frank is a Ph.D. candidate at UT Austin specializing in human–robot interaction. He has engineering experience at Dow Chemical, NASA, and Argonne National Lab, and serves as a Robotics & Software Architect Advisory Board Member for SmartCafe. His research focuses on enabling intuitive, AR-based communication between people and robots operating in complex environments.

Rohan Polavaram

CTO and Co-Founder

Rohan holds a B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from UT Austin and has software engineering experience at CCC Intelligent Solutions and AGNC Lab. He is currently a Robotics Research Engineer in the Nuclear and Applied Robotics Group, working on mixed-reality interfaces and human–robot teaming in challenging environments.

Arjun Nair

COO and Co-Founder

Arjun earned his B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from UT Austin and previously worked as a software developer intern at BizCloud Experts. He is now a Staff Research Engineer with the Nuclear and Applied Robotics Group, developing AR-driven control systems and scalable, intuitive multi-robot interaction frameworks.

Ayan Chaudhry

CAIO and Co-Founder

Ayan holds a B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from UT Austin, with experience at Harman International and AMD. He is a Research Engineering Scientist Associate in the Nuclear and Applied Robotics Group, creating HRI solutions that enhance collaborative autonomy in complex operational environments.
